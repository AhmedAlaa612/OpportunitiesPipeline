The applications are now open for the **European Parliament Schuman Traineeships 2026**. Candidates from around the world, including European & Non-European nationals, are eligible to apply. The Traineeship is for **5 months.** Each year, the European Parliament selects around **1000 Schuman trainees.** The Schuman traineeships are paid and can be undertaken at one of the European Parliament’s official places of work – **Brussels**, **Luxembourg**, and **Strasbourg** – or in its Liaison Offices in the Member States. There is no application fee. More details are below.

## **Quick Overview**

* **Location:** Europe
* **Host Organization:** European Parliament
* **Program:** Schuman Traineeships
* **Main Cities:** Brussels, Luxembourg, Strasbourg
* **Duration:** 5 Months
* **Deadline: 31st May 2026**

### **Traineeship Period**

The Traineeship will take place from 1 October to 28/29 February.

### **What are the Schuman Traineeships?**

The Schuman Traineeships are officially paid trainee programs offered by the European Parliament to help young graduates gain practical experience in European institutions.

The European Parliament offers traineeships in areas such as law, communication, finance, multilingualism, administration, IT, politics, and public policy.

### **Financial Benefits**

Selected trainees receive:

* Monthly stipend of €1889 (euros)
* Travel allowance of 300 euros.
* Professional work experience
* Access to the EU institutional environment

### **Number of Traineeships**

* 1000/year.

### **Why This Traineeship Matters**

This opportunity allows trainees to:

* Gain experience in a major EU institution
* Work in an international environment
* Build professional networks across Europe
* Improve policy and communication skills
* Learn how EU institutions operate

### **Where will you work?**

You can complete your Schuman traineeship in one of the European Parliament’s three places of work—Brussels, Luxembourg, or Strasbourg.

### **Traineeship Fields**

You can choose from around 500 traineeship offers per campaign, in the following fields:

* Communication
* Law
* Multilingualism
* International Relations
* Administration
* Infrastructure and Logistics
* EU Internal Policies
* Information Technology (IT)
* Human Resources (HR)
* And many others

### **Eligibility Criteria**

Applicants generally must:

* Be at least 18 years old
* Hold a university degree
* Have strong knowledge of one EU language
* Have good knowledge of another language
* Have not previously worked for an EU institution for more than two months
* Meet the traineeship requirements of the selected position
* Some traineeships may also accept applicants from non-EU countries.

### **Required Documents**

Applicants may need:

* CV (Europass format may be required)
* Motivation letter
* Degree certificates
* Language proof
* Criminal record certificate
* Supporting documents

### **How to Apply for the European Parliament Schuman Traineeships 2026?**

1. Please visit the **[Traineeship Application Page](https://www.europarl.europa.eu/at-your-service/en/work-with-us/traineeships)**
2. Select and apply for a maximum of **three** traineeships.
3. If you are selected, you will get an official Admission Letter via email.
4. The official link is given below.
