# Orange Corners Nigeria Incubation Programme Cohort 15 for young Entrepreneurs (30,000 Euros in funding)
**Category:** Call for Applications
**Application Deadline: May 30, 2026**

## Summary

Applications are now open for theOrange Corners Nigeria Incubation Programme.Launched in 2019 by the Kingdom of the Netherlands and implemented by FATE Foundation, the programme empowers young entrepreneurs aged 18-35 to build sustainable ventures.

*1*

Application Deadline: May 30, 2026

Requirements

* The Orange Corners Nigeria Incubation Programme are now open for Ogun-based entrepreneurs aged 18–35, sectors like Circular Economy, Agric, Health, Renewable Energy, Tech & Water.

**Benefits**

6-month programme equips you with the tools to succeed:

### Enterprise Management Training

Master business fundamentals through interactive workshops on strategy, finance, and marketing.

### Business Incubation & Advisory

Receive tailored guidance and support to scale your venture, including industry visits.

### Funding Support

Access up to €30,000 for prototype development and testing to bring your idea to life.

### Mentorship & Networking

Learn from experienced entrepreneurs and connect with local and international markets.
