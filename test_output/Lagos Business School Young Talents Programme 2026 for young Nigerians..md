Applications are now open for the **2026 Lagos Business School Young Talents Programme.**  The Young Talents Programme (YTP) is a highly selective talent development initiative designed to identify, develop, and connect outstanding young graduates across Nigeria. The programme brings together high-potential individuals for an intensive **two-day, in-person learning and leadership experience** focused on preparing participants for life and career opportunities beyond academia.

YTP challenges participants to think critically, collaborate in teams, develop capstone projects, and present innovative solutions to real-world problems. The programme goes beyond traditional training by combining academic excellence with professional exposure, leadership development, and career positioning.

At its core, YTP is built on three pillars:

* **Mentorship:** Participants receive faculty-led and professional mentorship that continues after the programme through structured team-based mentoring lasting approximately six months.
* **Talent Development:** The programme stretches participants beyond their academic comfort zones, helping them build leadership capacity, problem-solving skills, and future-ready competencies.
* **Talent Sourcing:** YTP serves as a pipeline for identifying exceptional young talent for advanced academic opportunities, research pathways, and recruitment by partner organisations and sponsors.

Participation is highly competitive, with about **50 participants selected annually** from a large national applicant pool. The programme targets young graduates who demonstrate strong academic performance, leadership potential, and readiness for accelerated growth.

Eligibility Requirements

* First Class or Second Class Upper degree holders
